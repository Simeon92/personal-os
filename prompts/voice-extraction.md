# Voice Extraction Prompt

**What this does:** Analyses samples of your writing and produces a voice profile you can paste directly into `01_context/voice-and-style.md`.

**How to use:**
1. Copy the prompt below
2. Paste it into any AI tool (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Claude.ai)
3. Add 5-10 samples of your writing — emails, Slack messages, documents, anything you've written at work
4. Paste the output into `01_context/voice-and-style.md`

---

## PROMPT

I'm going to share samples of my writing. Analyse my voice and communication style, then produce a structured voice profile I can use to brief an AI writing assistant.

Look for:
- How I structure arguments (do I lead with the point or build to it?)
- Sentence rhythm and length (short and punchy vs. longer and discursive?)
- What I lead with in different contexts
- Any distinctive phrases, transitions, or patterns I use repeatedly
- What I avoid (formality, hedging, jargon?)
- How my tone shifts across contexts (email vs. Slack vs. document)
- How I handle complexity — do I simplify it, embrace it, use frameworks?

Here are my writing samples:

[PASTE YOUR EMAILS, SLACK MESSAGES, DOCUMENTS HERE — aim for at least 5 samples across different contexts]

---

Produce a voice profile with exactly these sections:

**Core Voice**
One paragraph describing how I communicate — the overall register and feel.

**Voice Principles**
3-5 principles that define my writing. Each as: [Name] — [One sentence description].

**How I Actually Communicate**
Bullet list of specific observations about my patterns — the more specific the better.

**Anti-Patterns — Never Use**
Things that would sound wrong coming from me. Group into: corporate buzzwords I avoid / filler phrases / hedging language / anything else notable.

**Mode Adaptation**
A table showing how my voice shifts across contexts:
| Mode | Compression | Formality | Key Rules |

**The Voice Test**
One sentence that captures how to check if something sounds like me.
