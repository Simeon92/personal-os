Review 01_context/idea-log.md and 01_context/backlog.md. It's Monday — start of a new week.

1. Flag any ideas parked 3+ weeks: "Still relevant? Promote to sprint, keep incubating, or archive with a note?"
2. Surface 1-2 ideas that connect to current priorities in current-week.md or that a recent blocker may have unblocked
3. Check backlog for anything that should move into this week's sprint

Keep it brief. The goal is to make sure good ideas don't rot, not to review every item in detail.
