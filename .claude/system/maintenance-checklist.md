# Maintenance Checklist

*Hygiene checks that keep the system trustworthy. Run during monthly audit and weekly retro.*

---

## Monthly Checks

**1. Stale Drafts (02_projects/)**
Scan files older than 30 days. For each: ship, archive, or delete. Drafts that sit become avoidance, not WIP.

**2. Orphaned Ideas (idea-log.md + backlog.md)**
Flag ideas parked 3+ weeks with no review. Still relevant? Promote to sprint, keep incubating, or archive with a note.

**3. Commitment Hygiene (commitments.md)**
- Commitments with no due date: add one or remove
- Completed commitments not yet cleared: move to Completed
- Items with no update in 2+ weeks: surface and resolve
- Pattern check: 3+ slipped commitments this month = flag in audit

**4. People Page Freshness (03_people/)**
Flag people pages with no interaction logged in 30+ days. For active colleagues, this suggests meetings aren't being captured. For external contacts, consider relevance.

**5. Sprint Carryover (current-week.md)**
Count tasks carried over 3+ consecutive weeks. Either: break them into smaller pieces, escalate whatever is blocking them, or acknowledge they're not actually a priority and move them.

**6. Session Learnings Review (04_learnings/session-04_learnings/)**
Scan for patterns not yet promoted to working-preferences or mistake-patterns. Compile: 2+ similar entries = pattern worth naming. Archive processed files older than 8 weeks.

**7. Folder Structure Integrity**
Check documented structure exists. Flag files in wrong location. No files should live in the root of personal-os outside the folder structure.

---

## Weekly Retro Quick Checks

- Commitment health: How many made this week vs. kept?
- Pillar balance: Where did time actually go?
- Goal connection: Which monthly milestones did this week advance?
- Learning capture: Any session learnings worth promoting?
- Avoidance patterns: Anything deferred 2+ sessions in a row?

---

## Quarterly Deep Clean

- Archive completed sprint files older than one quarter
- Review all backlog items — kill anything parked a full quarter without resurfacing
- Refresh people pages for key stakeholders
- Review working-preferences for outdated or contradictory items
- Check CLAUDE.md is still accurate
- Tool review: 15 minutes checking new capabilities in connected tools, MCP servers, and Claude Code features

---

## The Maintenance Mindset

A system you can't trust is worse than no system. Every file should be either current, archived with context, or deleted. Nothing should rot. The monthly audit is not a ceremony — it's quality control.
