## How This Works

1. **Capture:** When Claude notices a commitment in conversation, it gets logged here with an ID (C-XXX format)
2. **Surface:** Every Morning Briefing checks for overdue and due-today commitments
3. **Complete:** When fulfilled, move to Completed with date. Clear at weekly retro.
4. **Patterns:** Weekly retro tracks commitment health — made, kept, slipped.

**If something is overdue:** Claude names it in the next briefing without shaming. The goal is to keep them visible, not to create anxiety.
